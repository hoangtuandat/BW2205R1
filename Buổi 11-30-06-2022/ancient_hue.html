THE JOURNEY INTO HUE'S SOUL

Ancient Hue Garden Houses is an elegant collection of five Imperial Garden Houses located in Kim Long village, adjacent to Hue’s Imperial Citadel. As an ideal gateway to the city’s magic, Ancient Hue Garden Houses offer a journey into Hue’s authentic mandarin residential area and lifestyle where tradition, poetry and spirit have been preserved over time.
